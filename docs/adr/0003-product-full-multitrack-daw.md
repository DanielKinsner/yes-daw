# 0003. Product: a full general-purpose multi-track DAW

**Status:** accepted · 2026-06-23 · Deciders: Dan

YES DAW is a full, general-purpose **multi-track DAW** (Logic / Pro Tools / Cubase / Sonar class) —
audio and MIDI co-equal, linear-timeline core — **not** a stem-separation or finishing/mastering tool.

The three research reports recommended a narrow stem/finishing wedge, but that advice assumed a small
team with no existing tools. It doesn't apply: the owner already ships standalone Demucs-stemming and
mastering-DSP apps, which stay standalone and individually tuned, and may integrate later **only via
plugin/tool interfaces** — never by merging codebases. The owner currently has no DAW and wants a
legit one they own end-to-end.

See [the direction brainstorm](../brainstorms/2026-06-23-direction-brainstorm.md). This supersedes the
"wedge" framing in `CONTEXT.md` and the original stem-centric roadmap (now rebuilt).
