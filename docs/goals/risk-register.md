# Risk register — the road from H13 to alpha and beyond

> Living table (ADR-0037 follow-up). Each risk carries the **earliest mechanical signal** that it
> is going wrong — a check or event, never a feeling — and the standing mitigation. Update at
> horizon kickoff/close; prune what dies, add what the loop discovers.

| # | Risk | Likelihood / Impact | Earliest mechanical signal | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| R1 | **Green in CI, dead on real hardware.** Headless `underruns==0` and fake-device gates don't transfer to real drivers/interfaces | High / Fatal to alpha | A [reality-lane](../reality-lane.md) smoke FAILs — or the log stays empty (no PASS ever recorded **is** the signal, and is the state as of 2026-07-03) | Reality lane: three one-command owner-machine smokes with committed dated results; H17 alpha gate runs on the packaged build |
| R2 | **Gates written to go green, not to bite** — the observed H1–H6 failure mode | High / High (false confidence compounds) | A mutation/adversarial pass finds a gate that cannot fail when its code breaks | Every H14–H17 plan has a "Gates that must bite" section naming negative controls at plan time; periodic adversarial review stays in the workflow |
| R3 | **FX parameter identity churn breaks saved projects** (one-way door: ParamIDs, value mappings, and FX chain rows enter user bundles) | Medium / Severe (user data) | The FX-era fixture bundle (frozen at first FX schema version) fails to open or re-render identically | ADR-0038 locks ParamID stability + normalized-value mapping rules; a forever-gate opens the frozen fixture bundle every CI run |
